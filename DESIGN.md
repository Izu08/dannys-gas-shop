# Design System: Danny's Gas Shop
**Project ID:** 15258688137055925964

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design is **Premium Utilitarian**. It combines the industrial reliability of a gas service with a modern, high-end consumer mobile app feel. It uses generous whitespace, subtle glassmorphism, and bold, high-contrast typography to create a sense of trust and efficiency.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Safety Orange (#ec5b13):** Primary brand color. Used for primary CTAs, active states, branding elements, and progress indicators.
- **Deep Charcoal (#221610):** Primary text and header color in dark mode. Provides strong grounding and a professional industrial vibe.
- **Off-White (#f8f6f6):** Main background color in light mode. Clean and soft on the eyes.
- **Warm Amber (#ff8c00):** Secondary accent for gradients and hover states to add vibrance.
- **Vibrant Green (#22c55e):** Success states and "Online" indicators.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Font Family:** Public Sans (San-serif).
- **Headers:** Bold to Black weights (700-900) for high impact and modern industrial feel.
- **Body:** Regular to Medium weights (400-500) for readability.
- **Character:** Tight letter-spacing on headers for a more "designed" look.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 
    - **Primary:** Fully rounded (pill-shaped) or generously rounded (12px) with a subtle drop shadow. Safety Orange background with white text.
    - **Secondary:** Transparent background with a thin border (1px) or subtle gray background (#f1f5f9).
* **Cards/Containers:** 
    - **Geometry:** Generously rounded corners (12px to 16px).
    - **Style:** Flat with thin 1px borders (#e2e8f0) or whisper-soft diffused shadows for depth. 
    - **Background:** Pure White (#ffffff) or ultra-light gray.
* **Inputs/Forms:** 
    - **Geometry:** Rounded-xl (12px).
    - **Background:** Soft light gray (#f8fafc).
    - **Focus:** 2px Safety Orange border.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Mobile-First:** Optimized for single-column scrolling.
- **Margins:** Consistent 16px to 24px side padding.
- **Density:** Spacious layout with clear section breaks to avoid clutter.
